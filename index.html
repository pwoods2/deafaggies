What is DeAF?

Howdy! We are an organization through Texas A&M that aims to promote the use of American Sign Language in the Bryan/College Station area and inform people about Deaf culture and history. We accept all skill levels (including beginners!) and work to adapt our meetings to fit the communnity and its needs. We have meetings to learn new signs and practice, as well as having socials to get to know each other and our community better. We hope to see you soon!
